Week 2 - Users, Administrators and Groups

--- Users and Groups ---

Standard user: one who given access to a machine but has restricted access to do things like install software or change certain settings.
Administrator (admin): a user that has complete control over a machine.

Windowns:
Get-LocalUsers
Get-LocalGroup
Get-LocalGroupMember Administrators

Linux:
sudo cat /etc/sudoers
cat /etc/password/
